Strenght & Weakness

Strengths

  • Problem-Solving Skills: Actively participates in coding contests on LeetCode and CodeChef.
  • Quick Learner: Adapts to new technologies and concepts rapidly.
  • Strong Analytical Thinking: Breaks down complex problems and finds logical solutions quickly.
  • Self-Improvement Mindset: Regularly engages in learning and upgrading skills.
  • Positive Approach: Open to feedback and continuously refines solutions.

Weaknesses

  • Overthinking: Tends to analyze problems deeply, leading to decision delays.
  • Dilemma in Complex Decisions: Struggles with selecting the best solution when multiple choices exist.
  • Time Management: Sometimes spends too much time on minor details.
  • Seeking External Validation: Often looks for expert suggestions, which can slow down problem-solving.
  • Fear of Imperfect Solutions: Hesitates to finalize solutions due to self-doubt.
