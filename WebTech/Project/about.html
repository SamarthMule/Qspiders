Our Story

Founded in 2025, NaturalGlow Soaps emerged from a passion for natural skincare and a commitment to sustainability. Our journey began in a small kitchen in Pune, where we experimented with traditional soap-making techniques using locally sourced ingredients.

Inspired by the rich heritage of Indian Ayurveda and the serene landscapes of the Chalke Valley, we aim to bring the purity of nature into every bar of soap we craft.

Our Values

  • Natural Ingredients: We use only plant-based oils, essential oils, and natural additives.
  • Sustainability: Our packaging is eco-friendly, and we strive to minimize our environmental footprint.
  • Handcrafted Quality: Each soap is made in small batches to ensure attention to detail and quality.
  • Community Engagement: We support local artisans and source ingredients from local farmers.
